I had the privilege of attending the JNIC (Jornadas Nacionales de Investigación en Ciberseguridad).
There, I was able to attend various talks where different research projects currently being
conducted in Spain regarding cybersecurity were presented.

In addition to cybersecurity, we were able to do some networking with different researchers from
other universities in the country, such as those from Castilla-La Mancha and Galicia.

Next year, we will be back with even more enthusiasm.
