Today, the reversea.me team had the pleasure of visiting the GSEC (Google Safety Engineering Center) offices in Málaga, where we had the opportunity to meet and exchange words with the VirusTotal development team in person.

During the visit, in addition to touring their facilities with sea views, we had a productive meeting where we presented our research directions to establish synergies between teams. It was a fruitful meeting that marked the beginning of a promising future collaboration between the university and Google. We are in touch!

Per aspera ad astra, but always remember that memento mori.
