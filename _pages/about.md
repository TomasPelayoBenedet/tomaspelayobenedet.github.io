My work sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and cybersecurity. I'm a Ph.D. candidate at the [University of Zaragoza](https://www.unizar.es), supervised by [Ricardo J. Rodriguez](https://webdiis.unizar.es/~ricardo/) within the [reversea.me](https://www.reversea.me) team, where I investigate how Machine Learning models and Large Language Models can be applied to real-world security problems, from detecting algorithmically generated domains (AGDs) to understanding what happens inside compiled binaries. On the binary analysis side, I collaborated with [Prof. Dr. Kevin Borgolte](https://kevin.borgolte.me/) at [Ruhr University Bochum](https://www.ruhr-uni-bochum.de/en), studying how intermediate representations affect LLM-based decompilation. Alongside my research, I also teach practical sessions for undergraduate engineering courses at the University of Zaragoza.

Outside academia, I co-founded [JnT Studios](https://https://www.linkedin.com/company/jnt-studios/) with [Juanjo Ruiz Borao](https://www.linkedin.com/in/juanjo-ruiz-borao-387928247/), a side project where we build custom software for small businesses and develop our own tools in our spare time. In parallel, I'm pursuing an MBA at the [International University of La Rioja](https://www.unir.net), because building something real requires more than good algorithms.

Before the Ph.D., I spent nearly two years at [Teltronic SAU](https://www.teltronic.es/en/) as a cybersecurity engineer, leading strategy and compliance for LTE product lines. I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science from the [University of Zaragoza](https://www.unizar.es) (graduated with honors in AI) and an M.Sc. in Cybersecurity Research from the [University of León](https://www.unileon.es).

Away from screens, I play bassoon, studied at the [Conservatorio Profesional de Música de Zaragoza](https://www.cpmzaragoza.com/) and currently first bassoon at the [Zaragoza City Hall band](https://bandaclubsocialzaragoza.com/banda-de-musica/). I'm drawn to the mountains, and when I'm not hiking the Pyrenees I'm usually running, bouldering, or rowing with the first team at [Os'Nabaters](https://www.facebook.com/p/Os-Nabaters-do-flumen-100071789451571/) in the Men's Eight.
